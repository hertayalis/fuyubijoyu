Pro Re-Issue Vaznev-9K | Warzone and Modern Warfare 2 Blueprint

The Pro Re-Issue Vaznev-9K is a Weapon Blueprint available in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (2022) and Call of Duty: Warzone 2.

It is a blueprint variant of the base weapon Vaznev-9K, one of the SMGs featured in Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2.
