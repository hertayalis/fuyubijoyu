General Hospital Spoilers: Ninas Autumn 2023 Exposure Confirmed Drew &amp; Carlys SEC Leaker F

General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal that a major revelation is about to rock Port Charles – and it’s sure to shake up Nina Reeves’ (Cynthia Watros) world once it happens. GH fans know Nina is the one who turned Drew Cain (Cameron Mathison) and Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) in for insider trading, so that secret has to come out.

Fall preview spoilers confirm that the truth will indeed be exposed this autumn, so it’s time to start thinking about Nina’s downfall.

Nina’s role as Carly and Drew’s SEC violation leaker will finally come to light, so she’s about to get busted and will have to live with the consequences.

Of course, things are currently looking up for Nina when it comes to her relationship with Willow Corinthos (Katelyn MacMullen), who’s open to having her birth mother in her life and getting to know her better.

Willow and Michael Corinthos (Chad Duell) are also allowing Nina to bond with Wiley Corinthos (Viron Weaver) again, so her dreams are coming true in the family department.

However, Nina’s engagement has hit a little bump in the road thanks to Sonny Corinthos’ (Maurice Benard) arrest.

Due to Sonny’s legal issues and all the other drama playing out, he’ll end up making a significant decision along with Nina.

Could that mean Nina and Sonny will move up their wedding timeline?

Although Nina wanted to wait until her relationship with Willow developed a bit more, Sonny’s situation may convince Nina that they need to seize the day.

Even if Sonny gets off the hook, Nina might still want to tie the knot ASAP and prove her devotion.

If Sonny and Nina’s wedding happens sooner than planned, that could put them on track for a fall ceremony – just in time for Nina’s secret to come out.

Nina and Sonny’s nuptials would be the perfect setting for the bride’s downfall!

Regardless, Nina will indeed face exposure sometime this autumn, so it’ll be interesting to see if “Eddie Maine” aka Ned Quartermaine (Wally Kurth) has total recall or if the truth comes out another way.

General Hospital spoilers say all of Port Charles will get updates on Nina’s SEC snitching eventually, so stick with us to see how that news will spill.
