Now that we’re up to date (kind of) on the Gigi Hadid boyfriend drama, can you tell us about her former relationships? Model and Next In Fashion host Gigi Hadid’s relationship list includes Joe Jonas and Tyler Cameron from The Bachelor among others.

Gigi had dated Joe Jonas for about five months in 2015 before breaking up with him in November of that year.
