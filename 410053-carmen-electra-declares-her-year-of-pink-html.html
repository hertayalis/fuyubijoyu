Carmen Electra Declares Her 'Year of Pink' Shortly after Being Photographed in a Distressed State

On the same day a tabloid posted photos of Carmen Electra in distress, the actress and OnlyFans content creator shared a color-coded version of her Y2K take on glamor.

Carmen Electra declared 2023 "the year of pink" on the last day of July with a playful carousel of her in various hues of the joyful color.

In the opening slide, she is in full glam with her long straightened hair hanging over her hot-pink crop top, slightly grazing the waistband of her matching formfitting maxi skirt. The delicate yellow-gold body chain is barely visible against her tan.

She showcases her range throughout the eight photos, which include her in a glittery pastel two-piece where she looks like a girl group member, sultry shots from the '90s/early 2000s, and concludes with her as the 'girl-next-door' in a coral bikini, grilling burgers.

Paparazzi Catch Carmen Electra in Vulnerable Moment

On Monday, Page Six ran photographs and a video of Electra sitting on the stoop outside her home in some distress. With her hair tied in a messy topknot, the icon seems to be breathing deeply and wiping away a tear.

Her rep assured the gossip site that there was "nothing to worry about" and said, "It looks like she had a migraine, and it's really hot out. The sun is basically in her eyes!" She is dressed casually in a band shirt with camouflage cargo pants and sneakers.
