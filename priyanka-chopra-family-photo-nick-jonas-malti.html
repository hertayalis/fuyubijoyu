Priyanka Chopra Shares a Sweet Family Photo with Nick Jonas and Baby Malti

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas are the picture-perfect family.

The Quantico star shared a new photo of their family of three on Instagram earlier this week. In the sweet picture, Chopra, who is wearing a mint green set emblazoned with a black-and-white geometric pattern, lies on a rug while holding her 11-month-old baby girl, Malti, on her chest. Malti, clad in a white baby onesie, reaches for her mother's face as proud dad Nick Jonas looks on from the side. Wearing corduroy pants and a gray-and-brown long-sleeved shirt, Jonas casually lies on his side while observing the mother-daughter duo playing.
