Madge Sinclair Net Worth

Madge Sinclair was a Jamaican-American actress born on April 28, 1938 in Kingston, Jamaica. She was best known for her roles as Nurse Ernestine Shoop on six seasons of Trapper John MD and Bell Reynolds in Roots. She also provided the voice of Sarabi in the 1994 Disney film The Lion King.

About

Played Nurse Ernestine Shoop on six seasons of Trapper John MD and portrayed Bell Reynolds in Roots. She also provided the voice of Sarabi in the 1994 Disney film The Lion King. 

Before Fame

She was a promising stage Actress in New York City's black theatre productions. 

Trivia

She was nominated for an Emmy Award for her role of Belle in the groundbreaking miniseries Roots, and played Queen Aoleon in Coming to America. 

Family Life

She was married twice, and had two sons with her first husband, Royston Sinclair.
