Tragic Orange County Bar Shooting Claims Lives of Tonya Clark, John Leehey, and Glen Sprowl Jr.

A horrifying incident unfolded at the Cook’s Corner pub in Orange County, leaving three innocent lives lost and several others injured. The victims of this tragic Orange County bar shooting have been identified as Tonya Clark, John Leehey, and Glen Sprowl Jr. The community is reeling from the shock of this senseless violence that occurred on the premises.

The Orange County Sheriff’s Department Coroner Division publicly released the names of the victims on Friday. Glen Sprowl Jr., 53, of Stanton; Tonya Clark, 49, of Scottsdale, Arizona; and John Leehey, 67, of Irvine all lost their lives in the violent incident that unfolded at the Trabuco Canyon Cook’s Corner pub.

Injured Victims and Recovery

Six individuals were injured in the shooting, with two initially classified as critical. As of the latest updates, one adult male was shot in the chest and an adult female was sent to UCI Medical Centre in Orange, both in critical condition. Four other male adults were reported to be in stable condition. Three of them have since been released, while one is recovering from surgery for an arm wound.
