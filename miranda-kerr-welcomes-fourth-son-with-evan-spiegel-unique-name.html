Miranda Kerr welcomes fourth son with Evan Spiegel find out his unique name

The former Victoria's Secret Angel shares a son with ex Orlando Bloom

    Congratulations to Miranda Kerr and Evan Spiegel! The couple have officially welcomed their third child together, Miranda's fourth, and it's a boy once again!

    The model, 40, shared the good news on her Snapchat page, posting a photograph of his new Milo baby blanket from Naked Cashmere and a pair of yellow socks for her newborn son, named Pierre Kerr Spiegel.

    She wrote: "We are overjoyed by the arrival of our little ray of sunshine, Pierre Kerr Spiegel. We couldn't be more excited to welcome our fourth son into our family. Feeling so very blessed."

    The former Victoria's Secret Angel and her husband, 33, are also the parents to sons Hart, five, and Myles, three. Miranda welcomed a son with her ex-husband Orlando Bloom as well, Flynn, who turned 12 last month.

    From 2010 to 2013, Miranda was married to the actor, who is now engaged to Katy Perry (with whom shares daughter Daisy Dove Bloom, now three). Since 2017, the Australian model has been married to her billionaire CEO husband.

    In a 2022 Vogue Australia interview, she affirmed that co-parenting with Orlando and Katy was seamless and she found great joy in being a part of their blended family.

    "Co-parenting with Orlando and Katy is something that I feel really happy about," she told the outlet. "From day one when Orlando and I decided to separate, I said, 'Listen, whatever we do, we have to put Flynn's needs first, not our own.' That's the way we make our decisions. Family is my number-one priority."

    She continued: "I just love being a mother and I always wanted three boys, so I feel really blessed that I have three healthy boys," although when asked if she wanted more, she replied: "I feel like I'm open, so we'll see what God decides."

    "No matter what, if we're deciding on holidays or what to do, it's, 'what is in the best interests of Flynn?' really, and that is a way to put everything in perspective and make the right decision."

    In 2019, Miranda, while pregnant with Myles, told Marie Claire: "Flynn is madly in love with Hart! He really wanted a little baby brother so much. There hasn't been any jealousy or anything. It's been very sweet. And when Flynn walks in the room, Hart lights up like a light bulb."
