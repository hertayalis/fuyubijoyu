Freddie Roman Children: Meet Alan Kirschenbaum: – Freddie Roman was an American stand-up comedian, best known for his frequent appearances at “Borscht Belt” hotels.

Freddie Roman was born on May 28, 1937, in Newark, New Jersey, United States, and grew up in Jamaica, New York with his father who was a shoe salesman.

Freddie Roman became a teenage comic in small resorts including Caesars Palace on the Las Vegas Strip and Harrah’s Atlantic City, and continued to perform, well into his later years.

He co-wrote, and starred in, the stage show Catskills on Broadway, as well as appearing in several films. Freddie died in Boynton Beach on November 26, 2022, at the age of 85 from a heart attack. He’s survived by Ethel Kirschenbaum (his wife) and daughter, Judi Levin.
