#WeWantCody Movement Invades John Cenas Mysterious Instagram Shenanigans

The WWE Universe has made it clear that it is Cody Rhodes who deserves to finish his story in the main event of WrestleMania 40. While it looked like 16-time World champion John Cena preferred WWE’s creative direction for The Show of Shows, he found himself surrounded by the Cody supporters.

As we know, John Cena is fond of posting random images on his Instagram for people to decode their actual meaning. Cena once again took to his Instagram to post a picture of The Rock’s recent confrontation with his iconic cousin, The Tribal Chief on SmackDown.

This was the moment that has been perceived as Cody passing his spotlight to The Great One. Moreover, it seems that John Cena’s mysterious shenanigans point towards the match he wants to see at this year’s WrestleMania.

However, the #WeWantCody movement launched an invasion on Cena’s post, flooding the comment section with massive support for The American Nightmare.

Ever since WWE chose to insert The Rock into the WrestleMania mix against Roman Reigns, the wrestling fan base has expressed anger and displeasure towards the same, believing it is Cody Rhodes’ time to fulfill his dream. With the backlash in full force, we will have to wait and see what happens next on the road to WrestleMania.
