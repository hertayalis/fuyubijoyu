Ice Spices sexy slide show to Jelly has fans clamoring for new release

Ice Spice has risen to fame over the last year after her single “Boy’s a Liar” went viral. Since then she has released collaborations with Taylor Swift and Nicki Minaj.

Recommended Videos

The 23-year-old rapper released her first EP Like..? earlier this year in January, featuring hits like “Princess Diana” and “Bikini Bottom.” She has since shared with Billboard that she has plans to release a deluxe version of the EP this summer.

Fans have recently been anxiously awaiting a new release after Ice Spice teased an upcoming song via her TikTok.

The Bronx native shared a slideshow of photos of herself in a text thread over the lyrics “She shake it like jelly / hundred bands in Chanely.”

Fans are thirsting over the photos and the new song in the comments:

Fans are begging the singer to release the song ASAP even making suggestions on who she should collaborate with next.

Though she has yet to announce when she will be releasing new music, Ice Spice is keeping her schedule busy. She released “Barbie World” with Nicki Minaj, which is set to be featured in the Barbie film on July 21. She also was featured on Taylor Swift’s “Karma“ in June.

Not only that, but Ice Spice will be joining Doja Cat for select dates on her Scarlet Tour. Her appearances include Washington, D.C., Philidelphia, PA, and Brooklyn, NY.

While waiting patiently for new music, fans can keep listening to Ice Spice’s Like..?, and keep up with her life (and any new releases) on her Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok pages.
