Genshin Impact Dandelion Seeds: Lore, locations, uses and more

An auspicious item to the people of Mondstadt and a valuable resource for character ascension and crafting, dandelion seeds are quite hard to come by. Here's where you can find these seeds and how to use them in Genshin Impact.

First Published December 19, 2021, 16:16

Dandelions have a rich history with the Mondtstadt region and are an important item to have in Genshin Impact. As dandelion seeds are sacred to the people of Mondstadt not only for their uses like Dandelion Wine but it’s the perfect representation of their way of living in the region.

There are other notable uses for dandelion seeds that can benefit players if there know where to find the delicate flowers. In this guide, we detail everything you need to know about this revered flower, the best spawning locations as well as how to farm their seeds and more.

What are dandelion seeds in Genshin Impact?

Dandelion seeds are a local speciality item that is quite hard to farm due to their in-game respawn rate. According to lore, “A tiny seed that rides on the wind. Even without its feathered wings, it still holds the hope from afar within”.

Dandelions are region-exclusive items to Mondstadt except for a vendor in Inazuma that sells these seeds. Furthermore, it has crafting and ascension uses that can be beneficial to players who have specific characters on their roster.

Where to find dandelion seeds in Genshin Impact?

Travelers should be pleased to know that they won’t have to travel far or wide in the pursuit of dandelion seeds. The seeds, as well as the flowers, can be found in the wild throughout Mondstadt, however, there are a few places that have high concentrations of dandelion seeds and flowers.

Once harvested, dandelion seeds and flowers take three real-world days to respawn in the farmed area. Additionally, the NPC vendor, Karpillia, sells dandelion seeds for which she can be found at the Sailing Breeze Shop on Ritou Island, Inazuma. You can buy up to five portions of dandelion seeds for 60,000 Mora each every three days.

You can make use of the official Teyvat Interactive Map to find the exact locations of dandelion seeds across the Mondtstadt region. There is one location known for its high concentration of dandelion seeds which is situated near the Mondstadt Gates.

You can also find a cliff located southwest of a lake near Dawn Winery that has a good concentration of dandelion seeds. It will take some time to get to this area, but you can easily get up to 10 dandelion seeds here.

To farm dandelion seeds from the flower, you must strike the plant with an Anemo attack for the flower to dislodge the seeds before farming them. It is best then to have Anemo characters in your party like Sayu, Venti and Kazuha.

How to use dandelion seeds in Genshin Impact?

As of writing, there are only three known uses for dandelion seeds in Genshin Impact. These seeds can be used when crafting Anemoculus Resonance Stone or potions like Gushing Essential Oil and Windbarrier Potion.

It is also widely known that both Jean and Eula make use of dandelion seeds for their respective character ascensions. Players who have rolled on Eula’s Character Event Wish banner and were lucky to obtain her should farm as many dandelion seeds required to level up the character.

We have covered everything there is to know about dandelion seeds from their in-game lore to the best farming locations and uses for your next visit to Mondstadt. Good luck, Travelers!
