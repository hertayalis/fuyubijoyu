See Hailey Bieber's Tropical Getaway with Justin Bieber

Hailey Bieber is escaping winter this year.

On Saturday, the model shared a photo dump on her Instagram from her weeklong tropical getaway with husband Justin Bieber and longtime pal Justine Skye. In the photos, she poses on the beach wearing a purple string bikini under a red striped cover-up, paired with a matching red checkered bucket hat. She also shared selfies of her cute braided hairstyle, sweet moments with Skye in a matching set of headphones, and—of course—more PDA with her husband, who kisses her on the cheek in a close-up.

Hailey captioned the series of images with a simple heart and palm tree emoji.

Giving a further glimpse into the fun weeklong trip, Skye shared a photo series of her own, in which she poses for a mirror selfie in a white string bikini, enjoys a round of shots with Hailey—among other fun captured moments. She and the model also pose for a shot in the water in coordinated triangle bikinis—Hailey in Janthee Berlin's Venice Top and Adela Bottom in Le Vert, Skye in complementary neon green.

"This past week was magical.. and also extremely hilarious. Grateful for these memories," she wrote in the caption. "Swipe for laughter."

Hailey has been experiencing her fair share of tropical weather recently. Last month, the model paid a visit to Miami for Art Basel and attended the Saint Laurent Rive Droite event in a pink-and-lilac floral-print bodysuit featuring a sweetheart halter neckline. She paired the look with understated rings and black square-toe heels.

Needless to say, the model has been keeping busy. On the same day she posted the photos from the trip, she was already in London with Justin for this year's Jingle Bell Ball, where he performed alongside musicians including Ed Sheeran, Becky Hill, and Jax Jones. Justin closed out the annual show with longtime hits "What Do You Mean" and "Sorry," as well as recent bops including "Peaches" and "Intentions."

On Instagram, the singer shared a series of photos from the brief visit with Hailey, featuring the two of them walking hand in hand on the streets while in coordinating tan and beige trench coats. In one of the shots, they also share a kiss in a telephone booth. "London Town wif my baby," he wrote in the caption.
