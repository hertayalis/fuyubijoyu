Sia Parents: Who Are Sia Parents?: Sia, officially known as Sia Kate Isobelle Furler is an Australian singer, songwriter, and voice actress.

Sia started her career at a very tender age and has been consistent throughout. She started her career as a singer in the acid jazz band Crisp in the mid-1990s where she collaborated with the band and contributed vocals to their album Word And The Deal and EP Delirium.

Following the disbanding of Crisp in 1997, Sia released her 1st studio album, OnlySee, and has since then released 9 more studio albums. She has received an array of accolades, including ARIA Awards, an MTV Video Music Award, and nine nominations for Grammy Awards.

In 2021, a laneway in Adelaide city center was renamed Sia Furler Lane, and a mural titled She Imagined Buttons was painted on a wall nearby to commemorate Sia’s 2011 performance in Adelaide.

As of October 2022, Sia has 15 entries on the APRA billion streams list, the most of any artist. She’s also an advocate for animal rights.
