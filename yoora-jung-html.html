Yoora Jung - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star who has gained fame for her self-titled YouTube channel. Her lifestyle entries often documenting her daily and weekly routines, including with beauty and fashion entries, have helped her grow to more than 127 million total views. 

Before Fame

She began posting to YouTube in January 2017. She graduated high school as the valedictorian of her class, and enrolled to study pre-med at the University of Notre Dame.

Trivia

She has amassed more than 1.5 million YouTube subscribers. She has also gained more than 240,000 followers. 

Family Life

She has one sister named Yoo Jin.

Associated With

She featured YouTube star Amanda Rach Lee in a photo she shared on her Instagram account in August 2019.
