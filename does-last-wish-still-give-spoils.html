Does last wish still give spoils?

Does Last Wish still give Spoils of Conquest?

Yes, Last Wish raid in Destiny 2 still gives Spoils of Conquest. However, it does not provide pinnacle gear like some other raids.

Can you farm Daughters of Oryx for Spoils of Conquest?

No, you cannot farm Daughters of Oryx for Spoils of Conquest. The best way to farm Spoils of Conquest is by repeatedly completing the Templar encounter in Vault of Glass or the Daughters of Oryx encounter in King’s Fall.

Where can you get Spoils of Conquest?

Spoils of Conquest can only be obtained by completing raid encounters in Destiny 2. They are not available from any other activities or sources.

Does Last Wish drop Spoils of Conquest?

Yes, Last Wish raid does drop Spoils of Conquest. However, it does not provide pinnacle gear like some other raids. You can earn 3 Spoils of Conquest from Last Wish raid.

Can you farm Spoils of Conquest in Last Wish?

No, you cannot farm Spoils of Conquest in Last Wish raid. The rotating legacy raids like Last Wish and Deep Stone Crypt only reward 3 Spoils of Conquest, so they are not the best raids to farm for Spoils of Conquest.

Is there a way to solo farm Spoils of Conquest?

No, there is no known way to solo farm Spoils of Conquest. Spoils of Conquest can only be obtained by completing raid encounters, which generally requires a full raid team.

What is the easiest raid to farm Spoils of Conquest?

There is no specific raid that is considered the easiest to farm Spoils of Conquest. However, repeatedly completing either the Templar encounter in Vault of Glass or the Daughters of Oryx encounter in King’s Fall is a good way to farm Spoils of Conquest.

How many Spoils of Conquest do you need to buy a red border?

To buy a red border for a craftable weapon that dropped exclusively from Raids, you need an additional 15 Spoils of Conquest per Deepsight Harmonizer. Once the Deepsight Harmonizer is selected, it will immediately convert the chosen weapon into a Red Border.

Do Spoils of Conquest drop from secret chests?

Yes, you can also get Spoils of Conquest from secret chests in raids. However, these chests can only be looted once a week on each character, so they are not the best source for farming Spoils of Conquest.

Can you get Spoils of Conquest without raiding?

No, Spoils of Conquest can only be obtained when completing raid encounters in Destiny 2. They cannot be obtained from any other activities or sources.
