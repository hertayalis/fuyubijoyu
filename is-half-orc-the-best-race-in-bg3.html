    What class should a half-orc be?

    The Half-Orc is well-suited to be a Barbarian, according to the article. Their strength and constitution are perfect Ability Score Increases, and they also receive Proficiency in Intimidation, which can be useful in social situations where the Barbarian typically has little to do.

    How strong is a half-orc?

    A Half-Orc has a +2 racial bonus to strength, which effectively means their bell curve for strength is higher than that of humans. This racial bonus makes them strong, and their combination of strength and durability can make them formidable in combat.
